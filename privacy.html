1. Information We Collect

Web Development Mastery is a free, open-source educational platform. We are committed to your privacy and collect minimal information. Here's what you should know:

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Payment or financial information (handled exclusively by Razorpay)
  • IP addresses, geolocation data, or device fingerprints
  • Browsing history or cross-site tracking data
  • Social media profile information

What We DO Collect

  • localStorage Data: We use your browser's localStorage to save your theme preference (dark/light mode), learning progress, and UI state. This data never leaves your device.
  • Session-Level Data: Temporary data such as active tab state or navigation preferences that are cleared when you close your browser.

All data stored via localStorage resides entirely on your local device. We have no access to, and no ability to retrieve, any data stored in your browser's localStorage.

2. How We Use Information

The minimal data we interact with is used exclusively to enhance your learning experience:

  • Theme Persistence: Remembering your preferred color scheme across visits.
  • Progress Tracking: Saving your learning progress locally so you can pick up where you left off.
  • UI Preferences: Storing sidebar states, font size preferences, and other interface customizations.
  • Interactive Exercises: Saving code editor states and exercise completion status locally.

We never transmit your data to any server. All functionality that requires data storage operates exclusively on your device through the browser's built-in localStorage API.

3. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to serve typography (Plus Jakarta Sans, Outfit). When you load our pages, your browser may connect to Google's servers to download font files. Google's privacy policy governs this interaction. Visit: Google Privacy Policy

CDN Services

Static assets may be served through Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) to ensure fast loading worldwide. These CDNs may log access requests per their respective privacy policies.

Razorpay (Donations)

If you choose to make a voluntary donation via our Razorpay link (rzp.io/l/Sanskar), your payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay. We do not receive or store your payment card information. Visit: Razorpay Privacy Policy

Gumroad

Digital products and resources may be available through our Gumroad store at ramsandesh.gumroad.com. Any purchases are governed by Gumroad's privacy policy.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Cookies

Our platform does not set first-party cookies. However, third-party services (such as Google Fonts or embedded content) may set their own cookies. We have no control over third-party cookies.

Local Storage

We use the browser's localStorage API to store:

  • Theme/color mode preferences
  • Learning progress and module completion data
  • Code editor states and exercise results
  • UI layout preferences (sidebar open/collapsed, etc.)

You can clear this data at any time through your browser's developer tools (Application → Local Storage) or by clearing your browser's site data. Clearing this data will reset your preferences and progress.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect, store, or transmit personal data to any servers, the security risks associated with server-side data breaches do not apply to our platform.

  • No Server-Side Storage: Your data never leaves your browser.
  • No Databases: We do not maintain user databases or authentication systems.
  • HTTPS: When hosted, our site is served over HTTPS, ensuring encrypted communication between your browser and our hosting server.
  • Open Source: Our codebase is publicly available on GitHub, allowing full transparency and community review of our practices.

While no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, the minimal data footprint of our platform significantly reduces privacy risks.

6. Children's Privacy

Web Development Mastery is an educational platform suitable for learners of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no risk of collecting data from children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction).

Our platform complies with the spirit of the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations by simply not collecting personal data from any user, regardless of age.

Parents and guardians can be confident that their children can use this platform safely. If you have any concerns, please contact us using the information below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. When we make changes:

  • The "Effective Date" at the top of this page will be updated to reflect the date of the latest revision.
  • Significant changes will be communicated through a visible notice on our homepage.
  • Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy. The version history of this policy can be tracked via our public GitHub repository.
